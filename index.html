Company Profile

Lorven Homes Private Limited

Lorven Homes Private Limited stands as a dedicated Manufacturer, Supplier, and Service Provider delivering advanced elevator solutions for diverse needs.

Our product line spans building passenger elevators, industrial elevators, residential mini lifts, manual outdoor lifts, hydraulic home elevators, elevator cabins, and MS elevator counter weights. Each product reflects a seamless blend of engineering precision, aesthetic appeal, and operational reliability.

Every elevator is developed with a space-optimized structure and robust load handling capacity. The weather-resistant build and low-maintenance systems promise longevity, while user-friendly controls allow effortless operation for all age groups.
